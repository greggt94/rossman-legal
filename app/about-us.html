Gregg Rossman

Gregg Rossman is an accomplished litigator with over 20 years of trial experience. As an assistant State Attorney, he has tried some of the most complicated and high profile cases in Broward County. Many of those cases have been featured in the local and national media. Gregg has been interviewed by ABC News on Good Morning America, CBS4 Miami, NBC6 Miami, WSVN7, WPLG10 Miami, and the Wall Street Journal, Sun-Sentinel and Miami Herald newspapers. Gregg has strong ties and connections in the law enforcement community. He is a second generation Miamian who has deep roots in the South Florida community.

Michael F. Spano

Mike’s main area of practice is Estate Planning. Mike can draft Wills, Trusts, Living Will Directives (such as a Durable Power of Attorney, a Health Care Power of Attorney) and Life Estate Deeds (Ladybird Deeds). Mike has prepared Summary Probate Administrations to assist families that have lost a family member prior to having a Will prepared. Mike provides his clients with carefully prepared estate plans that reflect each person’s unique goals. Mike recognizes that each client has different needs when it comes to both preserving and passing on their hard-earned wealth to their loved ones. He will listen. He will pay close attention to your needs and create a plan tailored to fit your situation. Mike is also well versed in Business Regulation, Business Formation, and Contract Law. Mike worked for years a Compliance Officer for an Investment/Insurance Company ensuring all financial regulations were followed. In that role he reviewed a broad array of contracts, rules, statutes and regulations. Mike can advise entrepreneurs on the best organization to utilize (LLC, Partnership, Inc.) and the best practices to be used to protect your business from liability. Mike is an experienced attorney with a depth of experience that truly cares about his clients.

Alexander Fischer

After trying over 40 criminal jury trials, Alexander Fischer transitioned to the civil side of law. Continuing to use his courtroom skills and experience, Alexander handles business and contract litigation for Rossman Legal. Before becoming an associate, Alexander was an Assistant State Attorney in Broward County for just under 6 years. While at the State Attorney’s Office, Alexander moved up quickly through the ranks and became a Felony Trial Unit Supervisor after just four years. While in that position, Alexander was in charge of supervising up to twelve felony attorneys and also handled cases in Miami and Palm Beach counties under Executive Assignment from the Governor. Alexander earned his Bachelor of Arts and Juris Doctorate from the University of Florida. After spending seven years in Gainesville, FL working towards his degrees, Alexander moved back down to South Florida, where he was born and raised. While Alexander’s focus is on civil litigation, he also assists Partner Gregg Rossman in the representation of police departments in Broward County. Whether it is forfeiture work, legal advising, or officer training, Alexander’s prior experience allows for an easy transition into this field. Furthermore, Alexander excelled in handling real estate law and representing associations after leaving the State Attorney’s Office. The Firm, Rossman Legal, handles Business and Contract Litigation, Contract Law and Negotiation, Probate, Trusts and Estate work, and Police Representation.
